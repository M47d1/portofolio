About Me

I'm Zainul Majdi, a cybersecurity enthusiast driven by curiosity and a passion for protecting digital environments. My focus lies in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and network security. I enjoy exploring vulnerabilities and crafting secure solutions to help individuals and organizations stay one step ahead of cyber threats. Through real-world simulations and hands-on projects, I aim to continuously sharpen my skills and contribute to a safer digital world.

Indonesian Derived Words Dictionary

A Java-based application that constructs a dictionary of derived Indonesian words using data structures such as trees and linked lists. This project aims to trace and display base words along with their derivations, although it currently has a few bugs in displaying some derived words.

Health Crisis Management Simulation

A text-based simulation that illustrates virus outbreaks and patient health management over a ten-day period. Players must make daily decisions to keep patients alive, facing challenges like daily HP reduction and limited medicine supplies. This project aims to educate users on health crisis management and timely decision-making.
